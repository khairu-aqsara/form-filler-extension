Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2025

1. Information Collection

Form Auto Fill does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or form data. All data generation and form filling occurs locally within your browser.

2. Data Usage

The extension uses the Faker.js library to generate fake data for form filling purposes. This data is not stored or transmitted anywhere.

3. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access and fill forms on the current tab
  • contextMenus: To provide the right-click menu for form filling
  • scripting: To inject necessary scripts for form filling

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
